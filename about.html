appDoption, the easiest way to find your next pet

appDoption is the brainchild of Charlie Grogan, Amy Moretti, Sherif Mahmoud, Jorge Becerill Hernandez and Haddassah Mendoza-Elias, created as part of a group project during their time at the Northwestern University Coding Bootcamp. We wanted to create an easy, picture focused way to see pets that are available for adoption. We believe that by being picture-forward, it encourages people to fall in love with animals they may otherwise ignore due to age, sex, or adoption location. Our goal is for you to scroll through, fall in love, and hopefully give that sweet face a new home.
